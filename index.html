About the Forum

We are bringing together researchers, entrepreneurs, and students interested in Earth observation. The meeting is a friendly, low-barrier opportunity to establish collaborations, foster networks, and strengthen partnerships. The target audience is people and companies based in and around Oxfordshire. Everyone is welcome, so do not hesitate to sign up—it’s free!
